IT College Okinawa

About

IT college Okinawa is located in the center and convinient environment of Naha City. The college area is surrounded with various facilities like hospitals, supermarket, clothing shop and food shops to ease day to day convinience. And also, the international street (the most famous tourist attraction place) is near the school and you can play and spend your time with friends.

Course

School's course can be two main courses which are Japanese Student Course (for international students require Japanese Proficiency Level 2 and above) and International Student Course.

Japanese Student Course

In this course includes four courses and are as follow.
1) Cyber Security Course
2) IT Specialist Course
3) Game Creator Course
4) Night IT Engineer Course (for workers who want to change their current job)
This course can apply Japanese Students but also International Students. But International Students must possess Japanese Proficienct Level 2 or abpve.

International Student Course

This course is composed of two sub courses and are as follow.
1) Japanese Business Course
This course can apply from overseas students.
2) Global Business Course
This course can apply overseas students (requires Japanese Proficiency Level 2) or language school graduate students.
